How have rankings changed over time? This tool visualises the long term stability of the top 10 institutions and the large variations of those ranked lower.

Each line on the visualization represents the evolution of a Business School according to their MBA programs ranked by the Financial Times. You can select institutions by country, by ranking in a given year or by name. You can also compare selected institutions according to different FT criteria.

Use the mouse on the vertical axes to filter the institutions per year (for example: institutions ranked between the 40th and the 60th in 2000). Use as many filters as you want!!
